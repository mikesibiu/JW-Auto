========================================
JW LIBRARY AUTO - INSTALLATION GUIDE
========================================

Version: 1.0.0
App: JW Library Auto for Android Auto

This app allows you to listen to JW.org content through
Android Auto while driving. Content includes:
- Weekly Meeting Content (This Week, Last Week, Next Week)
- Bible & Songs (Hebrew/Greek Scriptures, Kingdom Songs with vocals)
- JW Broadcasting (Monthly Programs + Governing Body Updates)


========================================
STEP 1: PREPARE YOUR PHONE
========================================

1. Open your phone's Settings app
2. Scroll down and tap "About phone"
3. Find "Build number" and tap it 7 times quickly
   - You'll see "You are now a developer!"
4. Go back to Settings
5. Open "Developer options" (now visible)
6. Enable "USB debugging" (optional, for ADB install)


========================================
STEP 2: ALLOW UNKNOWN APPS
========================================

1. Open Settings > Security (or Privacy)
2. Find "Install unknown apps" or "Unknown sources"
3. Enable it for your file manager app (e.g., Files, My Files)
   - On newer Android: tap your file manager app and toggle
     "Allow from this source"


========================================
STEP 3: INSTALL THE APK
========================================

Option A - Transfer via USB/Cloud:
1. Copy "JW-Library-Auto-v1.0.0.apk" to your phone
2. Use your file manager to locate the APK
3. Tap the APK file
4. Tap "Install" when prompted
5. If warned about unknown apps, tap "Settings" and allow

Option B - Download link (if shared online):
1. Open the download link on your phone
2. Download the APK
3. Open it from Downloads or notifications
4. Tap "Install"


========================================
STEP 4: ENABLE IN ANDROID AUTO
========================================

1. Make sure you have "Android Auto" app installed
   (Download from Play Store if needed)

2. Open Android Auto app on your phone

3. Tap the menu icon (hamburger or gear icon)

4. Go to Settings > Customize launcher
   OR Settings > General > App launcher

5. Find "JW Library Auto" and enable it
   (It might be under "Add media source" or similar)

6. If you don't see it:
   - Go to Android Auto Settings
   - Scroll to "Developer settings" (tap version 10 times if hidden)
   - Enable "Unknown sources"


========================================
STEP 5: USE IN YOUR CAR
========================================

1. Connect your phone to your car via USB or Bluetooth

2. Android Auto should launch on your car's display

3. Tap the music/media icon on Android Auto

4. Look for "JW Library Auto" in the list of media apps

5. Tap it to browse content:
   - Weekly Meetings
   - Bible & Songs
   - JW Broadcasting

6. Select any item to start playback

7. Use car controls for play/pause, skip, etc.


========================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
========================================

App doesn't appear in Android Auto:
- Make sure you enabled it in Android Auto settings
- Try enabling "Unknown sources" in Android Auto developer settings
- Restart your phone and try again

No content showing:
- Make sure you have internet connection
- The app fetches content from jw.org

Playback issues:
- Check your internet connection
- Some content may take a moment to buffer

Can't install APK:
- Make sure you allowed unknown apps (Step 2)
- Try downloading again if file is corrupted
- Check you have enough storage space
